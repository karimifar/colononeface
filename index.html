What is this?

It is an interactive experience to prove how fonts can change the entire impression of a piece of text.

The eyes and mouth of the drawing are typographic characters (:1) allowing the user to change their fonts and make different faces.

Fonts

The fonts used in the project are from Adobe, Google and your computer! (it detects some of the fonts you have installed on your system and adds them to the list)
